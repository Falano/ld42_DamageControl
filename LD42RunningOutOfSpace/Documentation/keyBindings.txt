Key Bindings, commands and instructions:

Protect the island from invasive plants (at least for 75 days).

use the buttons (left click) on the screen or:

left click-and-drag to move the view
right click to rotate; if on the right half of the screen, towards the right, if on the left half, towards the left
mouse wheel to scroll (hasn't actually been tested, might need speed adjustments)

0: end of turn
1: create rabbit (you need to click to choose its place on the board)
2: create cat
3: create fox
4: create eagle
5: create hunter
6: create ranger


space to toggle animals info screen
